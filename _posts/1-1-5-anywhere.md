Teachers `Show` with their class websites or LMSs.

So we made sure that Trinkets work with:

- Google Sites
- Wikispaces
- Canvas
- Weebly
- Edublogs
- Wordpress
- Sakai
- edX

Instead of videos, let students `Play` with a Trinket instead!
